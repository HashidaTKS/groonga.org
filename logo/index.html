The Groonga project provides logos for Groonga and Groonga related projects such as mroonga. You can use them under the CC-BY 3.0 license. Notation of the author is "The Groonga Project". You can also use a name of Groonga related project for logos of Groonga related projects. Please see also a description for a Groonga related project in the below.

If you are using Groonga on your project, please use our logo and spread Groonga and Groonga related projects! :-)
